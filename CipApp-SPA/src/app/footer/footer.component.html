Green initiative

Cyclopentane, a 100% green blowing agent, is being used extensively throughout the Americas and Europe. It is the green alternative to 141B which was discontinued in South Africa in 2016 and CIP industries cc, taking cognisance of enviromental imperatives , changed to cyclopentane in 2017.
